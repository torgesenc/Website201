Texas Fishing

Why the Lone-Star State attracts so many Anglers

Texas Fun Facts

  • The Texas state fish is the Guadalupe Bass
  • The Texas state record bass weighed in at a whopping 18.18 lbs
  • Texas is known for its gar fishing, a prehistoric fish that is the target of many anglers world-wide
  • While Texas has over 7,000 lakes, only one is natural, Caddo Lake in East Texas

Texas contains over 7,000 lakes, 191,000 miles of rivers and streams, and over 360 miles of coastline which makes it one of the most diverse fishing states in the US.

Texas sold about 2.1 fishing licenses in 2023, the most by any state
